What Do Neural Machine Transaltion Model Infers About Multiple Semantics of Homonyms?

Working with the Spoken Language System group at MIT CSAIL, we investigated how well a neural machine translation model infers multiple senses from homonyms. We empirically evaluated the quality of NMT representations for learning the different meanings of homonyms through an extrinsic meaning classification task.

Working with the Lab of Financial Engineering and Internet Policy Research Initative groups at MIT, we create a new cryptographic platform that allows multiple firms to compute aggregate measures of cyber risk without requiring any firm to disclose their own sensitive data on cyber attacks, penetrations, and losses.

Transfer Learning in Question Retrieval Tasks for Health Platform

I built an answer generator for a Q&A forum on SehatQ, an online health platform in Indonesia, by using transfer learning and domain adaptation techniques wrapped with machine translation model, trained on the MayoClinic Q&A forum.

Spoofing Alert Platform

We built a unified spoofing platform for traders that detects potential spoofing in the securities market and alerts traders and their managers when their actions appear like spoofing.

Who-to-follow Twitter Experiments
